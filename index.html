Charity donation and volunteer statistics

Purpose of this visualization is to explore trends of volunteering and donating charity of people with different age groups, gender

It could be interesting for people working in charity organizations and for curious people. It can help for campaigns of charity and volunteer, these statistics trends can narrow down their target audience.

  • Females have volunteered and donated more than males
  • people with University Diploma volunteered more and people with Post-secondary diploma have donated more
  • people with family income 125,000 and more have donated and volunteered more than others
